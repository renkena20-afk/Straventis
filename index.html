Restoring execution momentum in a deep-tech scale-up stalled by misalignment between technology readiness and organizational setup
  • Situation:
    The organizational setup and deployment expectations were ahead of the actual technology readiness level. Responsibilities overlapped, decisions were repeatedly revisited, and field activities did not translate into clear deployment progress.
  • Intervention:
    Assessed the technical readiness level and diagnosed the misalignment between technology maturity and organizational structure. Recommended targeted organizational adjustments and defined a structured field evaluation and sustainable market introduction strategy.
  • Outcome:
    Enabled leadership to restore execution clarity, realign the organization with technology readiness, and establish a credible pathway toward industrial deployment, predictable market introduction, and scalable growth.
