Selected advisory and leadership engagements

Restoring execution capability in a deep-tech scale-up organization stalled before industrial deployment
Situation: Technology had reached advanced maturity, but the organization remained structured for research. Responsibilities were unclear, field validation was not progressing, and deployment decisions were stalled.
Intervention: Assessed technical readiness level and diagnosed misalignment between technology maturity and organizational setup. Defined required structural adjustments, clarified deployment pathway, and established a structured field evaluation and market introduction strategy.
Outcome: Provided leadership with a clear roadmap and organizational framework to enable transition from technology development to industrial deployment.

Transforming a fragmented innovation landscape into a governed €500M+ growth portfolio
Situation: Innovation and product development activities were fragmented across multiple initiatives without clear strategic prioritization, limiting growth impact and resource effectiveness.
Intervention: Built and structured a comprehensive innovation and product portfolio, introduced governance and investment logic, and aligned development programs with strategic growth priorities.
Outcome: Enabled executive leadership to prioritize investments, focus resources on high-impact opportunities, and establish a scalable and strategically aligned growth pipeline.

Creating new growth opportunities beyond established core markets
Situation: Growth opportunities outside core markets were not clearly identified or validated, limiting expansion into adjacent applications and segments.
Intervention: Led structured market exploration and direct customer engagement to identify unmet needs, define product concepts, and validate new applications and value propositions.
Outcome: Established validated opportunity areas and defined product and market pathways forming the basis for future development and revenue growth.

Enabling successful market introduction of advanced material solutions in demanding industrial applications
Situation: Advanced material technologies required clear positioning, target application focus, and coordinated market entry to achieve commercial adoption.
Intervention: Defined global product strategy, target applications, value propositions, and customer engagement approach, aligning development and commercial teams.
Outcome: Enabled successful product introduction and commercial adoption across industrial and automotive applications.
